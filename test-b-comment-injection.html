Top 10 Tokyo Restaurants for 2026

Our curated guide to the best dining experiences in Tokyo this year.

1. Sukiyabashi Jiro Roppongi

The legendary sushi experience continues to set the standard. Omakase only.

Budget: ¥30,000-50,000 per person

2. Narisawa

Innovative "Satoyama cuisine" celebrating Japanese terroir. Two Michelin stars.

Budget: ¥25,000-40,000 per person

3. Den

Playful kaiseki by Chef Zaiyu Hasegawa. Known for creative presentations and warm atmosphere.

Budget: ¥20,000-35,000 per person

4. Florilege

Sustainable French-Japanese fusion with an open kitchen concept.

Budget: ¥18,000-30,000 per person

5. Sushi Saito

Three Michelin stars. Intimate 8-seat counter experience.

Budget: ¥40,000-60,000 per person
